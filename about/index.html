Brand Story

The Beginning

Bliss Beauty Plus was founded with a singular vision — to make luxury beauty accessible to every woman in Ghana and beyond, without compromising on ethics or quality.

Based in the heart of East Legon, Accra, we curate and offer premium cosmetics and personal care products that are 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and formulated to celebrate diverse skin tones and types.

Our Promise
